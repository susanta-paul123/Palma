About Palma

Palma offers property management services that provide comprehensive solutions, each tailored to meet specific requirements and objectives. Our sole objective is to guarantee utmost convenience to our local and international clients while ensuring their property delivers maximum yield with minimum hassle.

The management services offered to our clients include dealing directly with prospects and tenants, marketing rentals, collecting rents, handling maintenance and repair issues, responding to tenant complaints.

Palma’s services also extend to Community Management and the day to day operations of maintaining our developments to ensure they remain iconic and continue to grow in value for its property owners. Palma currently runs the community management for Silverene Towers in Dubai Marina, which is highly reputed for its upkeep, recording high growth values for its owners.

In line with Palma’s objective to offer a world-class living experience, Palma has devised a selection of premium services, including a Bespoke Interior Design Service, Home Management and Handover Services. Click on the below to download the service card for each.

CEO'S MESSAGE
