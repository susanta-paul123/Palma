Our Philospophy

Palma is a world-class, bespoke developer and one of the most renowned and prestigious names in Dubai’s real estate industry since 1998. Palma has built its impressive $ 2 billion portfolio project by project, taking on one at a time to ensure that each is given the absolute focus and attention of the company’s team of meticulous experts.

With its discerning and quality-centric approach to development, Palma has completed 12 iconic projects that stand as exceptional additions to Dubai’s ambitious skyline, including Olivara Residences & Park, Silverene Towers, Infinity Cayan Tower, The Jewels, Dorrabay, La Residencia Del Mar, German International School Dubai and its flagship project, Serenia Residences, the only exclusively residential community on The Palm Jumeirah’s Crescent.

In 2022 Palma Holding plans to launch an extraordinary beachfront residence on the most unique plot on the Palm’s Crescent. This will be a 2.5B (~ $700M) residential development that will dazzle its prospective residents.

Development Management Services

The key focus of the Development Management Services division at Palma is to support investors in their quest to develop outstanding properties. Staying true to our core values and a commendable track record of being a solid foundation, our development management division aims at taking investor’s projects from land selection all the way to handover of units.

This includes sourcing the right plot, site investigations, conducting feasibility studies, raising capital, developing a design, working and managing architects, consultants and contractors, legal & accounts management, construction works, sales & marketing and asset management.
