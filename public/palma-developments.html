Developement Management Services

The key focus of the Development Management Services division at Palma is to support investors in their quest to develop outstanding properties. Staying true to our core values and a commendable track record of being a solid foundation, our development management division aims at taking investor’s projects from land selection all the way to handover of units. This includes sourcing the right plot, site investigations, conducting feasibility studies, raising capital, developing a design, working and managing architects, consultants and contractors, legal & accounts management, construction works, sales & marketing and asset management.
